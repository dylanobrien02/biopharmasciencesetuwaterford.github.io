THE COURSE

HOW LONG IS THE COURSE?

Bachelor of Science (Honours) Molecular Biology with Biopharmaceutical Science is a 4 year course

WHAT MAKES THIS COURSE UNIQUE?

This is a unique course that offers an interdisciplinary curriculum combining molecular biology and biopharmaceutical science. It emphasizes practical training, drug development, and prepares graduates for diverse career opportunities in the pharmaceutical industry. It may also provide opportunities for cutting-edge research and industry collaborations, offering a comprehensive and specialized education for students interested in the interface between molecular biology and pharmaceuticals.

SEMESTERS

Your college duration is made up of 8 semesters through the 4 years youll be studying

Year 1: Semester 1 and Semester 2

Year 2: Semester 3 and Semester 4

Year 3: Flexible Semester and Semester 6

Year 4: Semester 7 and Semester 8

ENTRY TO THE COURSE

HOW TO APPLY

The entry route for this course is from BSc of Science (Hons) (Common Entry) When you are finished year 1 for this course you must select (Molecular Biology with Biopharmaceutical Science) as your degree option and you will be guranteed a place in this course

MATURE APPLICANTS

If you are a mature applicant and want to study this brilliant course you must fill out the CAO form before either 1 February or the late application deadline on 1 May.

ENTRY POINTS FOR CAO APPLICANTS

The entry points for this course if you are applying through the CAO are 289 - 589 points

CAREER OPPORTUNITIES

CAREER OUTCOMES

Graduates can work as research scientists in the biopharmaceutical industry, conducting experiments, analyzing data, and contributing to the development of novel drugs and therapies. They may also work in quality assurance and regulatory affairs, ensuring compliance with regulations and quality standards.

Graduates can pursue careers in academia or research institutions as researchers, scientific writers, or science policy experts, contributing to cutting-edge research in molecular biology, biotechnology, and biopharmaceutical science, or shaping policies related to biopharmaceuticals and genetic engineering.

CAREERS IN WATERFORD

The field of BioPharma in Waterford offers an abundance of exciting opportunities for recent graduates With companies such as EirGen Pharma headquatered in Waterford and Eurofins BioPharma headquartered in Dungarvan, The demand for skilled professionals in areas such as drug development, quality control, and regulatory compliance provides ample chances for career growth.

The supportive ecosystem of established bio-pharma companies in Dungarvan and Waterford, along with the availability of cutting-edge research facilities and a skilled workforce, makes it relatively easy for graduates to find employment in this dynamic industry.

CAREERS IN IRELAND

Ireland has emerged as a prominent hub for bio-pharma and molecular biology jobs in recent years, offering a plethora of opportunities for professionals in these fields. The bio-pharma industry in Ireland is well-established, with numerous global pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies having a significant presence in the country. These include companies such as Pfizer, Johnson and Johnson, AbbVie, and Amgen, among others.

Jobs in molecular biology in Ireland can be found in various settings, including academic and research institutions, biotech startups, and pharmaceutical companies. Roles may involve genetic research, DNA sequencing, gene expression analysis, protein engineering, and drug discovery, among others. One of the advantages of pursuing bio-pharma and molecular biology jobs in Ireland is the availability of cutting-edge research facilities and state-of-the-art laboratories. Ireland also has a strong academic community.

CAREERS ABROAD

Bio-pharma and molecular biology jobs are available in various countries worldwide. For example, Dubai has emerged as a global hub for life sciences and biotechnology, attracting international companies and professionals for research and development, quality control, and regulatory affairs roles. Other countries like the United States, Switzerland, Singapore, Germany, and Canada also offer promising opportunities in these fields, with major biotech hubs, renowned pharmaceutical companies, and cutting-edge research facilities.

It's important to note that job opportunities, regulations, and requirements may vary in different countries, and thorough research and understanding of local laws and regulations is essential before pursuing job opportunities in these countries or any other location.
